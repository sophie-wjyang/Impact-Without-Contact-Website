About

Why It Matters

On top of their existing medical conditions, residents in nursing homes are faced with the unnerving uncertainty of COVID-19. Extensive protective measures (such as restrictions on visitors and community events) have been put in place, which has resulted in heightened feelings of loneliness and depression.

Battling this pandemic with weakened immune systems and worsened spirits is going to be tough— but we want to do everything we can to help. Every conversation, every letter, and every phone call can make a world of difference.
