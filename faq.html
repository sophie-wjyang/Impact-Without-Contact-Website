Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does the signup and volunteering process look like?
    Check out our Volunteering Process page to get a full description of what volunteering with us will look like, from signing up to participating in initiatives.
  • Are there any requirements to apply?
    As we aim to make our opportunities as open and accessible as possible, the volunteer opportunities we provide are open to all demographics. For the majority of volunteer opportunities, no prior experience or specific skillset is necessary.
  • Is the volunteer recruitment process for initiatives competitive?
    While we try to make our volunteer opportunities accessible to everyone, sometimes the process for volunteer positions is competitive due to resource constraints. However, the application is always short and purely used to see if you would be a good fit for the specific volunteer initiative.
  • Will volunteer opportunities be in-person or virtual?
    Both! As restrictions are being lifted, we plan to host many in-person volunteering events, while still keeping lots of great virtual opportunities on our volunteer portal.
  • Will I receive volunteer hours?
    Yes! For volunteer initiatives held by our executive team, an outline of the number of hours you are eligible for will be given in the opportunity description. Upon completion of the volunteer initiative, our volunteer coordinator will be in touch with you via email to sign off on your volunteer hours. If you require your volunteer hours authorized at an earlier date, please email impactwithoutcontact@gmail.com.
  • How can I stay up to date with IWC initiatives and opportunities?
    We send our volunteers bi-monthly newsletters highlighting our initiatives and upcoming volunteer opportunities. Furthermore, you can stay up to date with IWC by following us on Instagram @impactwithoutcontact and/or joining our Discord server.
  • I have a question that wasn't answered here.
    If you have an inquiry, suggestion, or even idea for a new opportunity, feel free to shoot us an email at any time at impactwithoutcontact@gmail.com, or message us on instagram @impactwithoutcontact.
