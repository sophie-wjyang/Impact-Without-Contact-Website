Weekly News: 7/27/20 Canada

Hi, my name is Aishwaria, and I'm going to be a highschool junior. What I love to do, over my free time is bake, play badminton, embroidery, help people in whatever I can help them for, and also raise awareness to situations. So far for IWC, I have written blogs about COVID-19 news, a blog for recommendation, and also made cards and posters for various different groups. (True Davidson Acres, Michael Garron Hospital, Trillium Health Partner.) While writing this blog, I found some fascinating things that might interest you readers. Throughout my experience with IWC, I hope to gain more skills, and help people in need throughout these tough times!

Here are the latest news of this week:

Ontario has a significant increase of COVID-19 cases, with 195 cases on Friday July, 24, 2020. Doug Ford encourages people, like health officers, to use their power to make the number of cases each day reduce.
Read more:
https://toronto.ctvnews.ca/ontario-records-significant-surge-in-new-covid-19-cases-1.5037513

A British Columbia man was in hospital for 105 days, due to contracting COVID-19. However, he was able to survive, but some things were damaged. For example, his vocal chords, his weights, and also he got weaker.
Watch more at:
https://www.cbc.ca/player/play/1767452227703

More regions of Ontario, will be moving forwards to stage 3. The three regions that will not be moving are Peel Public Health, Toronto Public Health, and Windsor-Essex County Health Unit.The list of regions moving forwards on Friday 24, at 12:01 am are:
∙ Durham Region Health Department
∙ Haldimand-Norfolk Health Unit
∙ Halton Region Public Health

https://news.ontario.ca/opo/en/2020/07/ontario-moving-more-regions-into-stage-3.html

As students, and parents are waiting patiently to see if the school will reopen, or not, and the different protocol, it calls for it, will all be announced next week, by our education minister Stephen Lecce. Doug Ford wants to see students returning to school in the fall. However, many boards and educators are asking for more funds if opening schools. Here are some tweets:

Statistics of Covid-19 as of Friday July, 24, 2020; There have been 15,526,057 confirmed cases, 7,268,022 recovery, and 573,288 deaths worldwide. Top three countries are: the USA, Brazil, and India. As of last week, there has been an increase of 2.4 million confirmed cases.
