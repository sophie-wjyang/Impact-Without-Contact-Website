About

Why It Matters

As front line medical workers fight relentlessly against the pandemic, it’s up to the rest of us to support them. With a limited supply of materials, hospitals are not equipped to handle the massive numbers of infected patients. PPE in hospitals are what the medical staff use to protect themselves and minimize spreading the infection from one patient to another— which is why it’s so crucial that we do what we can to fill that shortage. Each time a worker comes into contact with a patient without the proper protection, they risk becoming infected.
