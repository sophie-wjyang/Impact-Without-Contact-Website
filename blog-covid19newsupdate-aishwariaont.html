Covid-19 + Schools: Ontario, Canada

Hi, my name is Aishwaria, and I'm going to be a highschool junior. What I love to do, over my free time is bake, play badminton, embroidery, help people in whatever I can help them for, and also raise awareness to situations. So far for IWC, I have written blogs about COVID-19 news, blogs for recommendation, and also made cards and posters for various different groups. (True Davidson Acres, Michael Garron Hospital, Trillium Health Partner.) While writing this blog, I found some fascinating things that might interest you readers. Throughout my experience with IWC, I hope to gain more skills, and help people in need throughout these tough times!

How will the province (Ontario) be reopening schools?

For the Ontario province they are looking into reopening schools for this September. However, parents have a decision on whether to put them in fully remote learning or to do both (In person and online).

How will elementary/middle schools be like this September?

Elementary/Middle school students will be returning to class as usual, 5 day per a week. As of now, the government keeps the number of students the same. This brings some parents into questioning, “Would my child be safe in an environment like this one?” Grade 3 and the grades below it aren't required to wear a mask, but are “encouraged.” However Grade 4- 12 it is mandatory to wear non-medical masks. There will only be one teacher for one cohort throughout the day.
