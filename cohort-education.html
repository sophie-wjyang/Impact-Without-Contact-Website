About

Why Education?

Covid-19 has impacted the quality of education millions of students receive. Virtual classes and assesments have made it hard to ask questions and stay engaged in class, causing many students to lag behind in their studies. We hope to offer affordable, accessible, and impactful education services to students around the world... all made possible by you.
