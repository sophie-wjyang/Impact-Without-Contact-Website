Impact Wellness

As a special holiday project, we created care packages for homeless shelters with food, clothing, and holiday cards.




Project Overview and Impact

Impact Wellness, IWCâ€™s fourth care package initiative, also marks our first in-person volunteering event! Over 40 volunteers from across the GTA gathered at Millennium Park for a day of outdoor care package assembling. Led by the Impact Without Contact Executive Team, volunteers assembled 1,700 care packages, which consisted of menstrual and food products.

The packages were then delivered to shelters across Ontario including Neighbour to Neighbour , The Salvation Army, Mission Services, and Interval House.



Additional Details

One volunteer hour was awarded for every card a volunteer created and submitted, with each volunteer being limited to submitting 20 cards. Furthermore, each submission gave the volunteer an entry into a giveaway for a $25 gift card and a water bottle from our sponsor, Figma.

Although this opportunity is now closed, similar remote volunteer opportunities will be offered in the future. Stay up to date with our initiatives by following @impactwithoutcontact on Instagram!
