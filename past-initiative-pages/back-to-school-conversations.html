Back to School Conversations




Project Overview

Premiered during back to school season, Back to School Convos is a series of IGTV videos to help students adjust to common online learning problems. The IWC executive team (comprised mostly of high school juniors and seniors) provide advice and resources to help students adjust to virtual learning in the 2020-2021 school year.



Watch the Series

  • Episode 1: How to Stay Motivated and Productive During COVID-19 with Volunteer Coordinator Lynn Shibata
  • Episode 2: Top Apps/Extensions for Productivity with Marketing Director Amy Peng
  • Episode 3: Strategies to Adapt to Online Learning with Care Centers Cohort Director Selina Qiu
  • Episode 4: Finding Volunteer Opportunities During COVID with Founder and President Sophie Yang
  • Episode 5: How to Beat Procrastination and Focus on Your Goals with Public Health Cohort Director Haleema Ahmed
  • Episode 6: How to be Organized Throughout the School Year with Fundraising Cohort Director Maria Khan
  • Episode 7: Reworking Your School Goals with Waterloo Subsection Director Namrata Mishra
  • Episode 8: Staying Connected With Others While Staying Safe with Waterloo Subsection Director Tiffany Yang
  • Episode 9: How to Stay Active During COVID with Marketing Director Catherine Tse
  • Episode 10: Managing a School Year Routine with Executive Director Jenny Wu
