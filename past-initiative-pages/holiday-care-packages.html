Holiday Care Packages




Project Impact

To spread the message of love and joy during the holiday season, the Impact Without Contact executive team put together 400+ holiday care packages complete with snacks, sanitary items, and clothing. 150+ volunteers from around the world created and digitally submitted over 1,700 holiday cards, which were included in the care packages.

The care packages were then delivered to residents at Homes First and Good Shepherd Ministries.



Additional Details

One volunteer hour was awarded for every card a volunteer created and submitted, with each volunteer being limited to submitting 20 cards. Furthermore, each submission gave the volunteer an entry into a giveaway for a $25 gift card and a water bottle from our sponsor, Figma.

Although this opportunity is now closed, similar remote volunteer opportunities will be offered in the future. Stay up to date with our initiatives by following @impactwithoutcontact on Instagram!
